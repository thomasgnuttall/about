About

I am a researcher in the Music Technology Group (MTG) at the Universitat Pompeu Fabra (UPF), Barcelona. My current research focus is the development of computational tools to assist in musicological tasks with a particular focus on melodic pattern recognition in Indian Art Music (IAM). Previously I have worked with Arab-Andalusian Music of the morrocan tradtion and AI generated music. Prior to my academic career I have worked on machine learning teams at Channel 4 Television, Glovo, Typeform and Crowdlinker, focussing on user-facing personalization and recommendation products. I am a praciticing musician involved in two Barcelona-based groups (1) Mukti (मुक्ति), a duo of myself and Jyoti Narang, mixing north indian folk singing with european dance/guitar traditions and (2) Awa pa los Calvos, a 7-piece band playing a mix of spanish rumba and funk/soul.
