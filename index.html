I am currently a researcher in the Music Technology Group (MTG) at the Universitat Pompeu Fabra (UPF), Barcelona, developing computational tools to assist with musicological research. I dedicate most of time to melodic analysis in Indian Art Music (IAM) but have previously have worked with Arab-Andalusian Music and on AI for automated music generation.

I am also a practicing musician involved in two Barcelona-based groups: (1) Mukti (मुक्ति), a duo of myself and Jyoti Narang, combining north indian folk singing with european dance/guitar traditions and (2) Awa pa los Calvos, a 7-piece band combining rumba flamenca with funk/soul.

Prior to academia, I worked on machine learning teams at Channel 4 Television, Glovo, Typeform and Crowdlinker, focussing on user-facing personalization and recommendation products.
