I am currently a PhD Candidate in the Music Technology Group (MTG) at the Universitat Pompeu Fabra (UPF), Barcelona, developing computational tools to assist with musicological research. I dedicate most of my time to melodic analysis in Indian Art Music (IAM) but have previously worked with Arab-Andalusian Music and on AI for automated music generation.

I am also a practicing musician involved in two Barcelona-based groups: (1) Mukti, a duo of myself and Jyoti Narang, combining North Indian folk singing with European dance/guitar traditions and (2) Awa pa los Calvos, an 8-piece band combining flamenco with funk/electronica.

Prior to academia, I worked on machine learning teams at Channel 4 Television, Glovo, Typeform, and Crowdlinker, focussing on user-facing personalization and recommendation products.
